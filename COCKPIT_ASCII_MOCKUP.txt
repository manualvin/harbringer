8. THRUST INDICATOR STATES
================================================================================

      ↑                        █                        ↑
    ← ◊ →                    ← ◊ →                    ← ◊ →
      ↓                        ↓                        █

Strafe Left:             Strafe Right:            Up Thrust:
      ↑                        ↑                        █
    █ ◊ →                    ← ◊ █                    ← ◊ →
      ↓                        ↓                        ↓

Down Thrust:             Multi-Thrust:
      ↑                      (Forward + Strafe Left)
    ← ◊ →                        █
      █                        █ ◊ →
                                 ↓

█ = Active (bright cyan #0088ff)
← ↑ → ↓ = Inactive (dark gray #2d2d2d)
◊ = Ship indicator (always visible)
